Status: 🟡 In Progress

## Exam Objective

cloud computing, its shared responsibility model and how cloud economics work. a little bit of serverless.

## Core Definitions

- Cloud computing: Delivery of compute, storage, networking, and software services over the internet on demand.
- Shared responsibility model: Security and operations duties are split between customer and cloud provider.
- Public cloud: Infrastructure owned and operated by provider, shared across customers, consumed via internet.
- Private cloud: Cloud-like environment dedicated to one organization, usually in its own datacenter.
- Hybrid cloud: Integration of public cloud and private/on-prem environments.
- CapEx (capital expenditure): Upfront spending on physical infrastructure.
- OpEx (operational expenditure): Ongoing spending based on usage.
- Consumption-based pricing: Pay only for resources consumed.
- Fixed pricing/commitment: Pay a predictable price for reserved or committed capacity.
- Serverless: Execution model where infrastructure management is abstracted and billing is usage-based.
